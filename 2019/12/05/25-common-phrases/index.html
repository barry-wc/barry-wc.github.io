25 common phrases

发表于 2019-12-05 分类于 English
No Phrases Explain Translate
1 24/7 constantly going on adv. <非正式> 一直不休（地），无时无刻（地）（指一天24小时一周7天不间断，读作 twenty-four seven)
2 Butterflies in my stomach to be nervous in a good way 七上八下；心里紧张
3 Draw the line to stop 划定最后界限
4 Easier said than done not as easy as it sppears to be 说来容易做起来难
5 Every cloud has a silver lining finding good in every bad situation 黑暗中总有一丝光明
6 Finding a needle in a haystack almost impossible to find 大海捞针
7 Fish out of water to feel out of place 如鱼离水；离水之鱼，不得其所
8 Go down in flames to fail suddenly and spectacularly 化为乌有
9 Get something off your chest to talk about something that has been borthering for a while 把心里的话说出来
10 Go the extra mile to make an extra effort 做比期望的多得多的努力
11 Plain as day obvious;clear (非正式)非常明显，明明白白
12 Pull yourself together to calm down and behave normally vi. 振奋起来，恢复镇定；控制自己
13 Sick and tired to be bothered and annoyed by something 十分厌烦
14 Sleep on it to get an extra time before making a decision v. 把问题留在第二天解决；考虑一晚上
15 Step up your game to start performing better 提高你的水平
16 Tip of the iceberg the small easily visible part of a larger problem 冰山一角；事物的表面部分
17 You rock an expression to compliment someone on something 你很棒；你酷毙了
18 Break a leg good luck 祝好运；大获成功（用于祝愿演员演出成功）
19 Throw under the bus to blame somebody for something 把（某人）当作替罪羊，牺牲（某人）
20 Under the weather feeling ill but not completely 人不太舒服；身体不适
21 Let’s play it by ear to act spontaneously and according to the situation 让我们随兴所至；我们见机行事吧；看情况吧
22 Think out of the box to think about something in a different way 打破陈规
23 Challenge accepted it used when someone claims that something is impossible 接受挑战
24 In the head of moment decision made in anger or passion 在最重要的时刻
25 Make a long story short to cut the details and get to the point 长话短说，简而言之；总之
